Timeline

  • Kim was born on October 21st 1980 to Robert and Kris Kardashian in LA.
  • She got married to Damon Thomas (music producer) at 19 (2000).
  • Kim's father passes away in September 2003, after a long battle with esophageal cancer.
  • Kim and Damon Thomas get divorced 4 years after getting married. Damon shared that one of the challenges with Kim was that she was a "compulsive shopper"
  • She becomes a personal stylist for Brandy, after she was labelled the worst dressed celebrity in 2004. She was also a personal shopper for stars including Lindsay Lohan
  • In 2004 she becomes involved with Brandy's brother, Ray J
  • In 2006 the Kardashians launch their first D-A-S-H store in Calabasas
  • Home video tape with Ray J leaks and goes viral in February 2007
  • 2007, Kim starts dating Reggie Bush
  • Keeping up with the kardashians first air showing the private lives of the Kardashian and Jenner family
  • In 2011, Kim marries Kris Humphries. The marriage only lasts for 72 days before they split up.
  • Kim's first born, North-West is born June 15th 2013.
  • Kim and Kanye West get married in May 2014
  • She releases her book titled 'Selfish' in 2015 and it somehow went on to be a New York Times Bestseller
  • North gets a little brother called Saint West December 5th 2015
  • Kim launches her first line of KKW Beauty which would continue to be her 3rd most successful business. In 2020 the business was valued at $1 billion
  • Kim's 3rd child, Chicago West is born in January 2018
  • Kim becomes interested in social injustice and begins studying law in 2018
  • Psalm West is born May 2019
  • Kim launches Skims, her shapewear brand. The brand was initially called kimono but it was changed due to public controversies.
  • Kim announces the end of KUWTK. The new Kardashian show is shown on Disney+
